Synopsis:

Tristin Hazel's life takes an unexpected turn when she literally bumps into a confident and charismatic man, only to later find out that he's her new lecturer, Dr. Hilman Ukasyah. Their first encounter is anything but pleasant, Hazel faces harsh criticism and even ends up in tears. Their relationship remains strictly professional until one fateful night when Dr. Hilman shows his protective side towards Hazel.

As Hazel starts to rely more on Hilman, she finds herself repeatedly in distress, with him always stepping in to protect her, promising to go to any lengths, even risking his life for her. But Hazel soon discovers that there may be more to this relationship than it seems. The night that changed everything was only the beginning, and as secrets unfold, Hazel begins to question whether everything is part of a larger plan, or if she's simply being deceived.
