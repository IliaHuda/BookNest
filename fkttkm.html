Description:

Fiqh Al-Awlawiyyat exposes the current generation of Muslims to things that should be placed on the first step, given priority and privileges, and things that should be put on the other side and done later. Failure to know the things and methods that need to be used will result in actions being disorganized and not neatly organized, thus delaying the ummah to achieve the desired goal.
