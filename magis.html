Synopsis:

The Halo Phenomenon pulled five individuals from different places and timelines into a magical world at the brink of collapse, where civilization is on the verge of fading into oblivion. Scattered across this enigmatic land, they each find themselves in extraordinary circumstances.

Saifudin encounters Sheikh Thauban, the sole guardian of what is believed to be the last surviving mosque. Tombiruo, now with a human appearance and strikingly handsome, falls for Juvia, a captivating Tur-Kus woman. Ryu-Watanabe is rescued by the Memalik, inhabitants of an underground city in Derinkuyu. Mikhail adapts to life among a pack of eloquent hyenas, while Carlos becomes a prisoner of war in Buyuk Bulsinatur, under the care of a mysterious woman named Cinnie.

As time draws them together, alliances shift. Among them, one succumbs to the darkness led by Tuan Turak, a powerful creature serving a sinister force even more terrifying. With the approach of Dukhan, the ominous thick smoke war becomes inevitable. Bound by fate, they battle relentlessly, knowing the cost could be their very lives.
