Synopsis:

The selfie has emerged as the newest cultural fad. Grab the camera, take a close-up of yourself, then, for added cuteness, pout your lips. That's what a selfie is all about a trend that has taken the world by storm. Airin has succumbed to the selfie obsession as well. She solely uses her lovely, glowing face and Barbie-doll-like eyes in every picture she shares on social media. When Airin observes her buddy Haness gaining popularity on Instagram, her obsession with selfies intensifies. Haness becomes well-known by giving Muslim women fashion advice, and her fan base keeps growing, which makes Airin envious.

Envious, Airin swears to use her favorite pastime taking selfies to get greater notoriety. She aspires to be the greatest Selfiestar and the queen of selfies. But Airin becomes more and more alone as a result of her quest for recognition. Now, Airin, the selfie junkie, is growing increasingly self-centered! Will Airin trade her virtual popularity for real-life friendships? Is this the price she must pay to become a Selfiestar?
