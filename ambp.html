Synopsis:

Tan Sri Hisham is looking to sell one of his estates! As soon as Fasha learns this, she rushes to claim the residence. To Fasha, having a house would put her on par with famous people like Justin Bieber, who managed to acquire a property at such a young age! Unfortunately, Fasha is unaware that Seri and her father already live in the home she wants. The former tenant, Nenek Elizabeth, graciously allowed Seri and her father to live in the Pink Mansion. Seri and her father, however, are no longer permitted to remain there after the elderly woman's death. When Fasha gets possession of the Pink Mansion, she shows off in style by hosting a tea party for her classmates. At this moment, Seri is forced to pose as her father's gardener and herself as a maid. While this is going on, Seri is planning how to ruin Fasha's visit and make sure she doesn't remain at the mansion for too long. She intends to make the mansion seem eerie and haunted, as well as to damage the tea party's cuisine. Seri just cannot bear to comply with all of Fasha's demands, which are as exacting as a princess's! Thus begins a rivalry over the Pink Mansion. Can Seri endure? Or will Fasha, with the help of The Wannababes, win?

Mungkin ini sebabnya nenek memilih untuk tinggal di banglo ini. Betul-betul menyerupai kediaman di tempat asalnya.
